JUAN DELICACIES

ABOUT

Juan Delicacies was founded by Ralph Lawrence C. Lim, having its first store in Malolos,Bulacan at the Cabanas in the year 2020 before the pandemic happened. It fueled his passion and motivation to execute his vision of owning a well-known and respected restaurant. He added a couple more iconic Filipino meals to his small eatery set-up just beside his own house. His small business gained a lot of attention to the netizens and was made viral on Facebook because of his generous amount of servings per meal ordered. Juan Delicacies became a must-try resto for a lot of food-lovers and netizens. Because of the growing community that adored his taste of cooking, he knew he had to expand to accompany more customers. He also had to hire crews. Then his first official restaurant at Cabanas opened. It opened so many doors for a lot of opportunities and business deals.
